About me

My name is Stacy Wandrey and I am currently studying Computer Science at University College Dublin. I am originally from the beautiful city of Chicago, USA. In my free time I like to explore and take pictures. I have a iPhone camera I mainly take pictures with (It's so convienient!!) but I also love taking polaroid photos. It's always a challenge to make sure the lighting is right and making sure the polaroid film is protected so that each shot comes out looking nice! Although, making mistakes can be fun too! Just like anything else, practice makes perfect and I hope to keep practicing my photography for many years to come!
