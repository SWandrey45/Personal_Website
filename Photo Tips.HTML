Photography Tips

You don't need a great camera to take great photos

In order to start taking photos, you don't need a camera that is the most technologically advanced model on the market! Getting started with photography means using something that takes photos!

What camera is best for you?
Are you Impatient? Use: Your Phone Camera If you are looking to get started right away, many smart phones already have a nice camera attached and it fits right in your pocket!
Are you a fan of vintage? Use: A Polaroid Whether you pick out a new model or find one in a second hand shop, polaroids are a fun way to practice taking photos with film.
Are you an experienced photographer? Use: A Digital Camera With lots of setting for light and focus, digital cameras can make taking impressive shots easy. However, learning to use one can be challenging!
Are you willing to make this hobby your life? Use: A Film Camera Film cameras require a lot of work and having access to a darkroom. You also have to learn about the different chemicals used to develop film.

Patience is key

Sometimes the best photos you take will happen at the right place and time. Sit on a bench for ten minutes and absorb your surroundings! You never know who will pass by or what sort of photo they will create for you. For example, I was standing on the edge of a pond when a dog ran by and scared a flock of pidgeons.
